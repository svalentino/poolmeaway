Extensions are not currently allowed to operate on "chrome://" URLs.

This means that this extension can not close/flush the socket pools automatically for you.

You have two options:

# Option 1
Allow extensions to operate on "chrome://" URLs.

Manually navigate to "chrome://flags/#extensions-on-chrome-urls" and set the value to Enabled. Once this is done, restart your browser.

# Option 2
Use this extention to just quickly reach the URL "chrome://net-internals/#sockets".

In this scenario, you will have to manually click the two buttons on your own.
